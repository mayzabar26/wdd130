The Powerful, Carrie White

Carrie is my favorite Stephen King book! It was his first work, and his writing has improved a lot since he launched Carrie in 1974. However, this book is the reason why I love his stories and also why I love horror stories. We all know that books are better than the movies, and I had this confirmation when I read Carrie a few years ago. In the movies we just have a stories about a shy girl whom was bullied at school, and out of the blue, she discovered superpowers. Sounds familiar like some movies we have out there, right? The book is so much more than that! It's about how powerful she really is that the whole town could feel and hear her, it's about the abusive relationship that her mother had, and it's the reason why she is the way that she is, it's about the vilians the their stories. It's about so much more! Now, let me tell you a little bit about the story. Without spoilers, of course!
